I am a philosopher. I completed my PhD in Philosophy at the London School of Economics. Previously I studied Physics and Philosophy at Brasenose College, Oxford. I was previously Departmental Lecturer in Philosophy at Pembroke College, Oxford. My research focuses on the philosophy and foundations of physics, in particular thermodynamics and statistical mechanics, and philosophy of science.

My thesis analyses the concepts of identity and indistinguishability in thermal physics.
